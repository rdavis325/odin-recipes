Beef Stew

Description

An Instant Pot recipe that's easy to cook and tastes pretty good.

It also healhy and comprised of mostly real food. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Beef Stock
  • Onions
  • Thyme
  • Tomatoe Paste
  • Garlic
  • Frozen Peas

Steps

  1. Cut the aromatics and beef.
  2. Season and sear the beef in two batches
  3. Soften the aromatics
  4. Cut the vegetables
  5. Add the seasonings and liquid
  6. Add the vegetables and seared beef
  7. Cook on HIGH pressure for 25 minutes
  8. Natural release pressure for 10 minutes and make the cornstarch slurry
    9. Natural release pressure for 10 minutes and make the cornstarch slurry
  9. Release the remaining pressure the thicken the sauce
  10. Season and Serve
