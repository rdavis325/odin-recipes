Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A simple but effective meal to crush your hunger

It's not the healthiest, but its amazing and worth it. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Spread Peanut Butter onto one slice of bread
  2. Spread Jelly on another slice of bread
  3. Cut sandwhich on the diangle
  4. Eat
